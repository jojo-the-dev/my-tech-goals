MY 2-YEARS TECH PLAN

At the start of the year 2024, I worked on my final year project. I was clueless on what to do and wasted all my time trying to debug a cloned project from GitHub which I eventually submitted. I got an E.

However, despite my frustration while working on the project and subsequent anger after seeing my grade, I realized I loved the hyper-focus I gave to the project and the pride I felt after solving each bug which I often worked on tirelessly for days.

I resolved, while working on the project, to continue doing tech for the rest of the year and kept to it even after my grade came out. It is June 30, 2024, and I am in the project phase of Zuri Training, and have reached Stage 1 in HNG Internship.

Now, I believe I need to have goals that would guide my tech career for the next two years and have a clear vision of my career path.

Firstly, my desire is to develop intensive skills in tech. In two years, I want to be extremely proficient in frontend technologies to undertake any project with minimal need for supervision and online research platforms e.g. YouTube, ChatGPT.

Secondly, it has been a childhood dream to develop software of my design and ComBox has been a pet project idea for years. By July 2026, I want to be skilled enough to design and build the frontend section of ComBox. Also, I want to be well-recognized in a community of tech professionals to make it easy to collaborate and build the full project, and also market it as a product.

In addition, I want to earn a minimum of $500 monthly working remotely as a frontend engineer.

Finally, I want to win a tech competition.
